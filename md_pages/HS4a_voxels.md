### Phantom description

This phantom is a segmentation of a ~2 mm isotropic MPRAGE acquisition
of a large female into 9 tissue classes:

- Air
- CSF
- Left teeth
- Right teeth
- Muscle
- Adipose tissues
- Bone
- Air internal
- Vitreous humor
- Brain
