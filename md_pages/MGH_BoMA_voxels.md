### Phantom description

This phantom is a segmentation of a 2 mm isotropic MRI anatomical
acquisition of a healthy male into 22 tissue classes:

- Air sinuses
- Adipose tissues
- Arteries
- Bone
- Brain
- Cerebellum
- CSF
- Ears
- Grey matter
- Head
- Humors
- Mastoids
- Nerves
- Nose
- Orbital fat
- Retina
- Skin
- Skull
- Spinal chord
- Subcutaneous fat
- Vertebral column
- White matter
